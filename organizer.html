BACHELOR OF COMPUTER APPLICATIONS (BCA)


BCA Course offered by Bosco Technical Training Society Affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi.

BOSCO Technical Training Society (A Unit of Don Bosco Group), Okhla affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Dwarka, New Delhi is one of the best institutes for BCA in Delhi NCR. It started 3 years degree programme in Computer Applications (BCA) in 2019. The institute is located in the heart of South Delhi, adjacent to Sukhdev Vihar Metro Station (On Magenta Line of Delhi Metro). The institutes is situated in sprawling lush green area of 10 acres. Don Bosco Group is spread across 132 Countries and has 2100+ Training Institutes world-wide, over 125 locations in India catering to skilling and employment of the needy youth. Don Bosco Delhi is providing technical and skilled training since 1971 in the areas of Computer, Mechanical , Printing and other short term skill training programs.

Bosco Technical Training Society, the best GGSIPU BCA institute in Delhi has state-of-the-art infrastructure and one of the best academia, which include experienced faculty, latest and high-tech computer lab, well stocked Library, Classroom with smart board etc.The Institute also has Football ground, Cricket ground, two Basket Ball courts, two Volley-Ball courts etc. , to help students in their all round development .

In three to four years of time Bosco Technical Training Society has plan to start the courses in the field of Management, Engineering, Law, Journalism and Mass-Communication etc. in affiliation with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIP University) Delhi.
